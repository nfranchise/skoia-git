Cabin Culture

SKOIA takes inspiration from the cabin lifestyle; a place for reflection, building relationships, and living sustainably. SKOIA infuses cabin culture into urban living.

SKOIA

[Skoÿ-yāh]

In late May 2014, while in the UK, Nick learned his post-grad job in California had fallen through. The next morning, he boarded a flight to Norway and with a small group of friends hiked to a cabin in the woods. It was there that the idea behind SKOIA was born. The name SKOIA comes from the cabin's name Røyrivannskoia: cabin on the water in Røyr.


Concerned with the lack of great gluten-free foods, Nick and Jordan set out to create the alternative. SKOIA aims to provide an exceptional food experience.
